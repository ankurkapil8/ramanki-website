Company Profile

Our clients are predominantly women residing in rural and semi-urban areas. We are dedicated to reaching the unbanked and providing crucial financial services to women entrepreneurs from economically and socially marginalized backgrounds. Our commitment extends beyond mere financial support; we also focus on empowering our clients with financial literacy to help them effectively manage their finances. At Fusion, we adhere to robust business practices and transparent policies, emphasizing a customer-centric approach. Our goal is to foster value and balanced growth for all stakeholders, with a firm commitment to placing our clients at the center of our efforts.

Mr. Devesh Sachdev
Founder, MD & CEO

VISION

Ramanki combines a strong social vision with a business orientation to empower underprivileged women by providing them with economic opportunities. Our aim is to enhance their quality of life through targeted support and resources that drive meaningful, transformative change.

MISSION

As a self-sustainable financial institution, we utilize our extensive distribution network to deliver a diverse range of products and services. This approach enhances our ability to efficiently reach and serve a broad client base while ensuring long-term financial stability and growth.
